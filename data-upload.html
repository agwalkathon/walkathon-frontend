CSV Format Reference

Upload a CSV file with external activity data. Duplicates are detected by strava_activity_id and skipped automatically.

strava_activity_id,strava_athlete_id,activity_name,sport_type,activity_date,distance_meters,moving_time_seconds,elapsed_time_seconds,elevation_gain,avg_speed
ColumnTypeRequiredExample / Notes
strava_activity_idinteger✅ Yes12345678 — unique ID, used for duplicate check
strava_athlete_idinteger✅ Yes98765432 — must match a registered participant
activity_nametext✅ YesMorning Walk
sport_typetext✅ YesWalk / Run / Hike / Ride
activity_datedatetime✅ Yes2026-06-14T06:30:00 (ISO format)
distance_metersnumber✅ Yes5230 (meters, not km)
moving_time_secondsinteger2400
elapsed_time_secondsinteger2600
elevation_gainnumber45.5
avg_speednumberm/s — e.g. 1.45 = ~11.5 min/km pace
💡 Tip: To convert km to meters multiply by 1000. To convert pace (min/km) to avg_speed (m/s): speed = 1000 ÷ (pace_min × 60).
