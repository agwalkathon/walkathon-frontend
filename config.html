Daily Cap & Special Days
21 km cap on normal days; selected dates count full distance
▼
Normal Days Rule:
Daily walk/run cap is 21 km. If a participant walks/runs more than 21 km on a normal day, recorded scoring distance will be capped at 21 km, worth 21 base points, and they will receive a flat +7 bonus points. Total = 28 points.
Special Days Rule Exception:
On selected special dates, the 21 km limit is removed. Participants receive 1 point per full kilometer, while bonus remains capped at +7 points. Example: 25 km = 25 base points + 7 bonus = 32 points.
